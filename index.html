Bank Transfer flow

Understanding the user and admin journey for bank transfer process

1. User placed a booking

2. Admin approved the booking

3. Guest received the booking confirmation email

4. Guest opens the payment page and selecting Bank Transfer as the payment method

5. Guest enter the payment details and uploads the payment proof

6. Guest is notified when the payment confirmation has been submitted

7. Admin will receive a notification email along with the link to the booking page

8. Admin can verify the payment by clicking the "VIEW" button

9. When opening the payment details, the Amount field will be automatically filled with the open balance amount

However since this guest is only paying for a down payment, I can change the amount to something else

10. When admin Verify the payment, guest will receive an email with the invoice attached
